Variables: names in code that have value given to them. 

Strings: are a type of variable that contain words and visual representation (uncalculated numbers) 
to what the variable is.

Functions: are rules that people use when they have things they want to put in and then receive a 
result after rules are followed

if statements are conditional rules (functions) that are executed/performed when certain rules are met 
in the statement 

Boolean Values: simple form, True and False statements.
